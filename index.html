About

I am a qualified Remedial Massage Therapist, having obtained the following qualifications to meet both UK and Australian standards in massage therapy

  • Professional Diploma of Sports Massage (London School of Sports Massage)
  • Diploma of Remedial Massage (NSW School of Massage)

I specialize in Remedial Massage, Pregnancy Massage, Sports Massage and Deep Tissue Therapy. Some of the techniques I use when massaging include Soft Tissue Release (STR), Muscle Energy Technique (MET), Neuromuscular Technique (NMT/Trigger Pointing) and Myofascial Releases (MFR)

Prior to working as a masseuse, I worked in the health and dental industries specialising in dental nursing, surgical assistance, sales and training.

I am an active person, competing in distance running events from 1500m up to, and including, the marathon. By actively participating in sporting events, I believe I have a greater understanding of Sports Massage and its benefits for athletes.

Massage Benefits

There are many reasons for people to seek out massage therapy. A massage will help your body relax, recover and feel rejuvenated. By assessing your body in its entirety, I will ensure that you get the best treatment for your individual needs

Below is a short list of benefits that can be achieved through massage therapy:

Improve Circulation and Lymphatic flow/drainage

Stimulate and balance the endocrine system

Restore balance to the parasympathetic and sympathetic nervous system

Maintaining flexibility in muscles, ligaments, tendons and joints

Stimulate or sedate nerve endings

Increase or decrease muscle length

Assist in removal of metabolic waste

Assist in the removal of scar tissue

Help reduce and alleviate pain caused by improper posture, muscle weakness and imbalances

Alleviate stress on musculoskeletal structures

Reduce oedema, fatigue, muscle spasm and leg cramps in Pregnant women

Improve body alignment and biomechanics

Promote recovery after muscular strain and injury
